What is COVID-19?


COVID-19 is a disease casued by the recently discovered coronavirus.

Many people infected by this virus will experience moderate respiratory illness and recover without treatment. People who are more vulnerable (e.g older people and people with underlying health conditions), are more likely to develop serious illness.

Prevention

To prevent infection and further transmission of COVID-19, do the following:

  • Wash hands regularly.
  • Avoid touching you face.
  • Maintain at least 1 meter from anyone outside your household.
  • Stay at home.
  • Avoid unnecessary travel.
